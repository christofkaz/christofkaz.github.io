Introduction

I created this webapp in Python using mostly the Flask module. The wares data is static therefore stored in Python dictionaries.

Thanks to session variables, the user can shop as if it were a real online store, no authentication required.

The frontend side of this webapp was mostly constructed with templates provided by Bootstrap

Store functions

The customer can browse through 5 different categories and add to the cart the items of their liking.

Once the customer is done shopping, he can proceed to the cart from where they can decide what to do next.

They are given an option to either increase the amount of the product they need, or just delete it from the cart

Processing the payment

To proceed with the order after confirming their cart, the customer is required to fill in the shipping and billing information to proceed with the payment

After the payment is processed, an email confirming the order and providing all the order details is sent to the customer.
